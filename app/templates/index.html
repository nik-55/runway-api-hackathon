This deployment runs on a cloud VM, and YouTube blocks yt-dlp requests from datacenter IPs (AWS, GCP, Azure) with a "Sign in to confirm you're not a bot" error. The URL field above will fail here — use the upload field instead. (Running ReelAgent locally? URLs work fine — feel free to ignore this notice.)

To grab a YouTube video as MP4 on your machine:

  • Easy — use a web downloader like vidssave.com (or any "youtube to mp4" site). Pick a 720p MP4.
  • CLI — install yt-dlp and run: 
    yt-dlp -f "bv*[ext=mp4][height<=720]+ba[ext=m4a]/b[ext=mp4][height<=720]/b" \
       --merge-output-format mp4 -o source.mp4 <youtube-url>

Then pick the resulting MP4 in the upload field above.
